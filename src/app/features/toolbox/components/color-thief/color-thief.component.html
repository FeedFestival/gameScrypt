PASTE TO EYEDROP

This tool is designed to help artists of any kind and even web developers to steal/eyedrop colors from images in a simple manner.

Step 0. Open the Snipping Tool in windows or any program like that and crop the image you want to take the colors from.

Step 1. Ctrl + C the image. Attention! The image itself.

Step 2. Paste it in the above field.

Step 3. Press the Eyedropper button and click on the desired color.
